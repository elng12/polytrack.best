Unblocked access + performance: open fast, run smooth

Trusted entry points and aggregators for unblocked access, plus compact performance tips to keep latency low and frames steady — even on school or office networks. Includes quick guidance for Google Sites hubs, Pizza edition packs, CrazyGames, and Poki.

1) 常见入口与聚合平台

Aggregators: CrazyGames and Poki usually offer better CDNs and caching. If reachable, prioritize polytrack on CrazyGames or Poki — typically optimized for first‑open speed and mobile fit.

Edu mirrors: polytrack Google Sites can serve as an accessible hub that jumps to the actual runtime. Pros: entry domains are rarely blocked; cons: jump chains change — watch for updates.

Derivative packs: sites like polytrack Pizza edition are community‑curated and update irregularly. Check versions and ad scripts before use to avoid degraded experience.

2) Unblocked 访问基本功

  • Open a “light page” (intro/placeholder) first to test reachability, then enter the game iframe to distinguish network vs loading issues.
  • If fullscreen fails, allow embedded fullscreen in browser settings or access under the same domain directly.
  • On mobile, prefer modern browsers with hardware acceleration and high‑performance modes enabled.

3) Polytrack Performance Tips（低延迟要点）

  • 保持前台：比赛时让游戏页面保持前台，避免浏览器后台限速造成的帧时间波动。
  • 扩展精简：关闭录屏、翻译、反跟踪等高占用扩展，减少输入延迟。
  • 窗口固定：避免频繁缩放窗口，分配重建会引入卡顿峰值。
  • 热管理：长时间游玩注意散热，热降频是“随机卡顿”的常见来源。

4) 访问失败时的三步排错

  1. 试域名：同平台下的不同入口可能分布到不同 CDN。更换入口域名测试（例如 CrazyGames 页 → 备用聚合页）。
  2. 试设备：更换成移动网络或另一台设备，以区分本机环境与网络环境问题。
  3. 试“轻页面”：先打开“说明/占位页”快速判断是否被阻断，再决定是否更换线路。

无论从哪个入口进入，访问稳定与帧时间稳定同样重要。把上面的 polytrack performance tips 固化成习惯，你会发现“可访问”与“可刷 WR”其实并不冲突。祝你一路顺风！如果你正在搜 polytrack unblocked 的稳定入口，本篇清单已经覆盖常见路径。
