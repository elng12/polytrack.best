About Polytrack

We blend creative sandbox tools with competitive racing energy

Polytrack began as a side project for friends who loved TrackMania-style loops and precise speed lines. Today it is a fully fledged browser platform where players design tracks, challenge global ghosts, and learn together.

Launch the game Contact the team

Our mission

Lower the barrier to high-skill racing. Polytrack aims to give every player a powerful track builder, smooth netcode, and coaching-quality feedback tools directly in the browser.

What we deliver

  • Creative tools: Modular editor with blueprint templates and ghost-driven debugging.
  • Competitive play: Live ghost racing, weekly challenges, and community leaderboards.
  • Education: Guides, analytics overlays, and masterclasses from top builders.

Product principles we follow

Readable speed

Every track communicates its racing line through lighting, camera, and terrain design.

Data-first coaching

Ghost replays, telemetry overlays, and segment splits keep learning loops fast.

Accessible everywhere

Polytrack runs in modern browsers with controller, keyboard, and touch support.

Who runs Polytrack

Polytrack is maintained by a small, independent team of racing game enthusiasts. We are players first — we build tracks, compete on leaderboards, and care deeply about the community that makes this game special. Our work covers everything from site development and content to community moderation and player support. If you'd like to get in touch, we answer every message within one business day.
