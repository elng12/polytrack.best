Privacy Policy

Polytrack.best is an independent guide website. This policy explains how this site handles logs, cookies, emails, advertising choices, and privacy requests.

1. Who we are

Polytrack.best ("we", "us", "our") publishes independent guides, troubleshooting notes, and safe launch links for Polytrack players. We do not operate the official Polytrack game client or game servers. For privacy questions about this website, email privacy@polytrack.best.

2. Information this website collects

  • Server logs: Basic request data such as IP address, browser type, requested URL, referrer, and time of visit.
  • Cookie preferences: Your consent choices for essential, analytics, and advertising cookies.
  • Contact emails: Your email address and message content if you contact us for corrections, privacy requests, or guide feedback.
  • Optional analytics: Aggregate page usage data when analytics cookies are allowed.

We do not create Polytrack game accounts, store official game profiles, process payment details, or collect official game leaderboard data on this website.

3. How we use information

  • Keep the website secure, available, and easy to navigate.
  • Reply to correction requests, privacy questions, and guide feedback.
  • Understand which pages need updates or clearer instructions.
  • Show advertising only where allowed by your cookie choices and applicable law.

4. Cookies and advertising

Essential cookies may be used to remember privacy choices and support basic site behavior. Optional advertising cookies may be used by Google AdSense after consent where required. Google and its partners may use cookies to serve and measure ads based on visits to this and other websites.

  • Manage site cookie choices with Cookie Settings.
  • Manage Google ad personalization at Google Ads Settings.
  • Read Google's advertising cookie explanation at Google's advertising privacy page.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data with service providers that help host the site, protect it from abuse, deliver email, measure site traffic, or display ads. These providers process data only for the services they provide.

6. Retention

  • Server logs are kept only as long as needed for security, debugging, and hosting operations.
  • Cookie preferences remain until they expire, are cleared by your browser, or are changed by you.
  • Emails are kept as long as needed to answer the request and maintain a correction record.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may ask to access, correct, delete, or restrict personal information that this website holds about you. Send requests to privacy@polytrack.best.

8. California privacy choices

We do not sell personal information for money. Some advertising cookies may be considered "sharing" under California privacy law. You can opt out by rejecting advertising cookies in Cookie Settings, using Google Ads Settings, or enabling a browser-level Global Privacy Control signal where supported.

9. Security

We use HTTPS and limit access to site administration tools. No website can guarantee perfect security, but we keep data collection small so there is less unnecessary personal information to protect.

10. Official game links

When you click the official game link, you leave Polytrack.best and visit a game client hosted outside this website. That destination may have its own privacy practices. Check the official publisher's notices for details about game-client data.

11. Changes and contact

We update this page when our practices change. For privacy questions, email privacy@polytrack.best or use the contact page.
